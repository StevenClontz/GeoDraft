About/Rules

To complete this Technology Challenge, your team must score at least 10 points on one of your iPads. Once your Deputy has confirmed completion of the Challenge, you will proceed to the afternoon Challenge Phase.

Points are earned by constructing the geometrical figures in the upper left corner of the screen using a virtual compass and straight edge. One point is awarded for constructing the figure, another for constructing the figure within an origin circle, and up to two more points for constructing the figure within a limited amount of moves.

You can hit Reset to return the number of moves to zero, and Undo/Redo moves just as easily. Use Layer to fade out complicated constructions to the background, and +/- to zoom in and out.

Tap the screen to begin. You can click About to see these rules again.
